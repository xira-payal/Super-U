About Us​

Super U Display Group is a China based custom store fixture factory, specializes in designing and manufacturing retail store fixtures and displays for the right-fit partners in all retail industries, enhancing your space environments and driving more sales for you. Whether you are a nationwide brand, a new startup, or a one-off project, Super U guarantees high-quality products, flexible designs and End-to-end service for you.

Our Services​
Estimation & Design

Our expertise work closely with you as the customer to fully capture your vision for custom retail fixtures, store displays, space furnitures, decoration, etc.

Planning & Engineering

From prototyping to engineering, we plan your store’s retail display fixtures in full collaboration with you to create the interior environments you want.

On-site Manufacturing

Our team combine technology and craftsmanship to build your custom store fixture and displays to your exact specifications in our 1,100,000 sq. ft. production facility.

Shipping & Installation

With our manufacturing facility located in Guangzhou and near all major routes and seaports, we can easily deliver and install retail display on time and on budget.
