About our COMPANY​

Yes, we are the shop fitting manufacturer factory. Established since 2006, our manufactory has 150 workers and we have a timber workshop and metal workshop over 20,000 sq meter with a full set of equipment, manufacturing facility with joinery, metal and paint utilizes the latest technology, allowing us to deliver on time, and can offer large capacity and competitive price for you.

Our Headquarters (Office and showroom) is based in Guangzhou. We set up a branch in Sydney for Turn-key service and maintenance since 2016.
